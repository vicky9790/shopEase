Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. A tracking link will be available once your item is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for most products. Please check the product page for return eligibility before placing an order.

How do I cancel an order?

To cancel an order, go to the "My Orders" section and select the item you want to cancel. If it hasn't shipped yet, you’ll see the “Cancel” button.

How can I contact customer care?

You can reach us at support@shopease.com or call us at 1800-123-4567.

Payments & Billing

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe, and cash on delivery (COD).

Is it safe to pay online?

Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and comply with RBI guidelines to ensure your transactions are 100% secure.

My payment failed. What should I do?

If your payment fails, the amount will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days. You can also retry with a different payment method.

Can I get a GST invoice?

Yes, you can request a GST invoice while placing your order. Make sure to enter your business GSTIN during checkout.
