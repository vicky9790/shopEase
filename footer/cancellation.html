Cancellation & Refund Policy

Learn about how to cancel your order and our refund process

Order Cancellation

How to cancel an order?

You can cancel your order before it is shipped. Go to “My Orders”, select the product and click “Cancel”. Once shipped, cancellation is not allowed, but you may return it.

Can I cancel part of my order?

Yes, individual items in a multiple-item order can be cancelled as long as they have not been shipped.

What if the item is already shipped?

Once shipped, cancellation is not possible. You can initiate a return after the item is delivered, depending on the product’s return policy.

Refund Process

When will I get my refund?

Once the cancellation or return is confirmed, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are made to the original payment method.

What if I paid via Cash on Delivery (COD)?

In case of COD orders, refunds are issued via UPI or bank transfer. We’ll request your payment details during the return process.

Are there any cancellation charges?

No, we do not charge any fees for cancellations made before shipping.
