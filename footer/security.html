Security & Protection

Your safety is our top priority — online and offline

1. Data Encryption

We use 256-bit SSL encryption across our entire platform to ensure that your personal and financial data is transmitted securely and cannot be intercepted.

2. Payment Protection

All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways like Razorpay, Paytm, and Stripe. We never store your card details on our servers.

3. Account Security

Your account is protected with OTP-based login and secure password protocols. We recommend keeping your password confidential and changing it regularly.

4. Fraud Detection

We actively monitor all transactions and user activity to detect and prevent fraud using AI-based threat detection systems.

5. Reporting Security Issues

If you suspect any suspicious activity or security vulnerability, please report it immediately to security@shopease.com. We investigate and take action swiftly.

6. Updates to Security Practices

We continuously upgrade our infrastructure and protocols to meet the highest security standards. This page will be updated as policies evolve.
