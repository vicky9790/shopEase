Shipping & Delivery

Know how and when your orders reach your doorstep

Our Shipping Policy

Where do we deliver?

We deliver across all major cities and towns in India. For remote locations, availability may vary.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping is available for select products with 1–2 day delivery.

Are there any shipping charges?

Shipping is free for all prepaid orders above ₹499. For COD orders or orders below ₹499, a small fee may apply.

How can I track my shipment?

After your order is dispatched, you’ll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track orders from your account dashboard.

What if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed beyond the expected delivery date, contact our support team and we’ll help resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
